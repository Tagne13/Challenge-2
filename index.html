About Me:

I graduated from Saginaw Valley State University in 2004 with a B.S. in Athletic Training. I then went on to be accepted into the physical therapy program at Oakland University and completed my Doctorate of Physical Therapy in 2009. My passions include learning, traveling, being outdoors in nature, and experiencing different cultures.

Work Experience:

I have worked as a licensed physical therapist since 2009 in various settings. I have approximately 4.5 years experience in outpatient orthopedics. Upon relocating to West Michigan in 2013, I transitioned into a homecare role as a physical therapist where I have worked with greater autonomy and effectively honed my time management skills.
